game rules
  • Pokemon ARHERA Edition is a turn based game!
  • Every player will start with 100 health and 50 stardust.
  • By attacking other player you will earn coins (20 per attack) which you will be able to exchange for stardust (20 coins = 20 stardust)!
  • You are allowed to recharge your stardust only 3 times!
  • Enjoy the game!
